About This Dashboard

This dashboard explores the global distribution of critical mineral deposits and highlights their importance for major industries such as electric vehicles, renewable energy, electronics, and aerospace.

How to Use It

Users can search by country, filter by mineral type, and select use cases to see how different minerals support key technologies. The charts update to reflect the current map view and selected filters.

Why Critical Minerals Matter

Critical minerals are essential for modern technology and the clean energy transition. Materials like lithium, cobalt, nickel, copper, and rare earth elements are used in batteries, wind turbines, solar panels, and advanced electronics.

Project Goal

The goal of this project is to create an interactive tool that helps users better understand where critical minerals are located, what they are used for, and why they matter in global supply chains and energy systems.
